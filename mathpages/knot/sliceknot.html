The \(\textbf{unknot}\) is the circle \(S^1\) and is denoted by \(U\).
the \(\textbf{unit 3-sphere}\) is denoted by \(S^3\) and is defined as: \[S^3 = \{(x_0,x_1,x_2,x_3) \in \mathbb{R}^4 | x_0^2+x_1^2+x_2^2+x_3^2 = 1\}\]. It is the unit ball in \(4\) dimentional space.
Two knots \(K_1, K_2\) are said to be \(\textbf{concordant}\), and denoted by \(K_1 \sim K_2\) if there is an embedding \[f : S^1 \times [0,1] \to S^3 \times [0,1] \] such that \[f(S^1 \times 0) =K_1, f(S^1 \times 1) = K_2\]
A knot \(K\) is slice if and only if there is an embedding of the disc into \(B^4\) with boundary equal to \(K\).
